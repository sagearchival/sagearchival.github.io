Sage Archival Consulting

Here to help you navigate your archival needs.

We offer subject-matter expertise in archival best practices to help plan, create, and support the continued operation of your institutional or personal collections. Our specialty is personal and family archives, ensuring your history is preserved for generations to come.

We believe archives should reflect everyone. We operate through a lens of social and disability justice to ensure archives are not a place for gatekeeping, but for preserving and amplifying historically marginalized voices. We deeply believe that everyone has a right to their own history and we are here to help you preserve it.

Services

  • Archival services

    We provide you with best-practice guidance on acquisitions and accessioning, arrangement, description and processing, and preservation of your institutional or personal records.

  • Practical advice

    At Sage, we focus on making archives more inclusive, accessible, and equitable. We don’t let perfect be the enemy of good and will work to ensure your archival needs are met with the resources available to you, regardless of size or budget of the project.

  • Policies, process, structure

    We help craft organizational policies to guide decisions meeting the unique demands of our field while following established best practices. We author processes that meet our clients’ high expectations. We advise on the organizational structure of your archive to help you determine when and what to scale as you grow.

  • Archival systems and solutions

    DAM got you down? Confused by CMS? We've got the technical expertise to help.

  • Oral histories

    We help you keep your stories alive. Whether conducting an interview, or establishing a program, we offer services to fit your needs.
